Each year, tens of thousands of pets are abandoned at their local shelter where staff and volunteers work diligently to reunite lost pets with their owners, re-home them or find rescue partners for these precious animals. Adopting a shelter pet is a economical, personally-satisfying alternative to purchasing a pet from a pet store. Find out more about adopting a cat with Cats Protection in our guide. Check out profiles and pictures of available animals at the shelter. We've got some awesome cats who are waiting for their new families to come and adopt them!
